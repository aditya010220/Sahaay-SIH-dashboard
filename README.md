Sahaay is a proactive, privacy-preserving digital mental health framework designed for Indian university students. It leverages multimodal AI and passive sensing from smartphones for the early risk detection of anxiety and depression. The ecosystem features an empathetic AI chatbot, a robust early warning system, and pioneering Indian Sign Language (ISL) support for inclusivity.

ğŸ¯ The Mission
University students in India face a critical mental health crisis, driven by immense academic pressure and pervasive stigma. This has resulted in a staggering 70-92% treatment gap, leaving millions without support. Our mission is to bridge this gap by creating a proactive, accessible, and confidential first line of defense, shifting the paradigm from reactive crisis management to preventative mental wellness.
